About

Hello, my name is Pedro Guitian. I am a sophomore at Marist College pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, with a concentration in Software Development and a minor in Cybersecurity. I am set to graduate in May 2027

Throughout my academic journey, I have honed my skills in programming languages such as Python, Java, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Additionally, I have cultivated expertise in database design and implementation

In Fall 2024, I collaborated with a team to develop Filtr, an MVP of a dating app designed to connect young individuals. My contributions included designing the database structure, implementing complex queries, and developing the matching algorithm that forms the core of the user experience. These efforts significantly enhanced the app’s functionality and user engagement.
